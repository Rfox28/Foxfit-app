HOW FOX FIT USES RPE
TARGET ZONE: RPE 7–8
Hard enough to stimulate adaptation, controlled enough to maintain quality across all sets.
RPE DROPPING? → ADD LOAD
If your RPE drops session-on-session at the same weight, your body has adapted. Time to progress.
RPE RISING? → HOLD LOAD
Same weight feeling harder means accumulated fatigue or poor recovery. Don't chase progression — consolidate.
RPE CLIMBING SET-TO-SET?
A 2+ point jump across sets signals fatigue outpacing recovery. Extend rest, reduce volume, or deload.
